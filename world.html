World Brief

This world is inspired by mythology and explores the real-world issue of cultural preservation. In this setting, myths and legends are literally fading from memory, and characters must navigate a world where ancient stories are at risk of being forgotten. Writers are invited to create characters, locations, and lore that fit naturally within this world.

World Guidelines

  • Contributors should create original characters, lore, or short stories.
  • Entries should respect the tone and genre of the world.
  • Keep submissions clear, meaningful, and intentional.
  • Submissions may be revised before final publication in the anthology.

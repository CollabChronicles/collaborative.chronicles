World Brief

This world is inspired by mythology and explores the real-world issue of cultural preservation. Myths and legends are literally fading from memory, and characters must navigate a world where ancient stories, beings, and ways of life are at risk of being forgotten.

The description of the world is intentionally vague. We Writers are free to write in whatever tone they would like. Inappropriate content however is not permitted. Keep content PG-13 -- we want to share our collaborative world with everyone:)

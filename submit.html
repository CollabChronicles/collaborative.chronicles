How to Contribute

We invite writers to create original characters, short stories, or lore entries for the current world. Submissions should reflect the world, genre, and theme described in the World Brief.

  • Keep entries meaningful, clear, and intentional.
  • Respect the tone and genre of the world.
  • There are no strict length limits, but aim for concise, engaging writing.
  • All submissions must be original and not previously published.

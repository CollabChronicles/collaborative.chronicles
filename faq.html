Frequently Asked Questions

Do I keep the rights to my work?

Yes, you retain full rights. Entries may be edited for formatting before publication.

Is this competitive?

No. While selected entries may appear in the anthology, the focus is on collaboration and creativity.

What happens to the proceeds?

All proceeds (minus estimated tax costs) from the anthology are donated to the charity associated with the current world.

Can I revise my submission?

Yes, you may revise entries during the submission period.

Do I need to be experienced?

No. We welcome writers of all skill levels as long as they engage thoughtfully with the world and theme.

Is Collaborative Chronicles a registered nonprofit?

No, we are not a registered nonprofit. Collaborative Chronicles is simply a personal creative project that hopes to help the world out through the power of literature. Proceeds are collected by the project organizer and donated in full to the selected charity.

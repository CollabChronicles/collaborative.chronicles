About Collaborative Chronicles

Collaborative Chronicles is a community for writers to build thoughtful fictional worlds together while helping out the real world! Contributors are encouraged to engage deeply with the world and create original characters and lore that truly belong to it.

What We Hope to Do

This project is just starting out, but here are the key actions we want to take to accomplish our goals

  • Gather writers from the internet to build a themed fictional world together
  • Once we collect enough submissions, create an anthology of the collected work
  • We will share this first anthology as a free ebook
  • On subsequent runs, based on response to this first anthology:
  • Publish and sell the anthologies electronically (most likely on Kindle Direct Publishing)
  • Donate 100% of the proceeds (royalties - estimated taxes) to a charity helping the real-world problem linked to the theme of the world
  • Create a community that allows writers to create collaborative, thoughtful, and meaningful work
  • Plan, collect writing, and create these anthologies cyclically
  • If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to reach out! -- collaborativechroniclesproject@gmail.com

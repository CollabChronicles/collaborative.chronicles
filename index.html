About Collaborative Chronicles

Collaborative Chronicles is a place for writers to build thoughtful fictional worlds together. Each project draws inspiration from a genre of storytelling and a real-world issue, and culminates in a published anthology supporting a related charity. Contributors are encouraged to engage deeply with the world and create original characters and lore that truly belong to it.

We value stories that are clear without being obvious, and meaningful without being opaque.
